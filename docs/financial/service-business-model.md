## Overview

Zixly operates as a service-based n8n consulting and integration business, focusing on Brisbane and South East Queensland SMEs. Our revenue model combines project-based fees with optional recurring retainers, providing flexibility for clients while ensuring sustainable business growth.

---

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Project Setup Fees

**Description**: One-time n8n platform setup and configuration
**Investment Range**: $2,500 - $5,000
**Typical Project**: 1-2 weeks
**Target Clients**: New n8n implementations

**Components**:

- Infrastructure setup and configuration
- Security hardening and backup setup
- Initial workflow templates
- User training and documentation
- 30-day support included

### 2. Hourly Consulting

**Description**: Custom workflow development and technical consulting
**Hourly Rate**: $150 - $250/hour
**Typical Projects**: 20-120 hours
**Target Clients**: Specific automation needs

**Components**:

- Custom workflow development
- Integration implementation
- Technical consultation
- Problem solving and optimization
- Training and knowledge transfer

### 3. Monthly Retainers

**Description**: Ongoing n8n platform management and support
**Investment Range**: $500 - $2,000/month
**Commitment**: Optional, flexible terms
**Target Clients**: Businesses wanting hands-off management

**Components**:

- Platform monitoring and maintenance
- Workflow optimization and updates
- New integration development
- Performance monitoring and reporting
- Priority support and troubleshooting

### 4. Stack Expansion Services

**Description**: Implementation of additional tools from self-hostable SME stack
**Investment Range**: $5,000 - $25,000 per tool
**Typical Project**: 2-6 weeks
**Target Clients**: Businesses ready to expand beyond n8n

**Components**:

- Tool selection and planning
- Installation and configuration
- Integration with existing n8n workflows
- User training and documentation
- Ongoing management (optional)
